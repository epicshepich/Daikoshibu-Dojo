Annual Registration Fee: $13

This fee is required to maintain registration with the Hakkō Denshin Ryū Federation. It helps pay for honbu operations and events such as the annual Tai Kai.

Monthly Training Fee: $25

This fee is required for members to train at the Daiko Shibu Dojo. It goes toward dojo upkeep, the dojo registration fee, and Sensei's registration, training, and testing fees with honbu.

Testing fees: $25

For all kyū grades, there is a $25 testing fee. This money goes toward the certificate and the new belt you recieve upon promotion. For all ranks higher than ikkyū, tests will be conducted at honbu, where you will be informed of the testing fees. When testing for gokyū or above, you will be required to take and pass a written test, which contains material from the Hakkō Denshin Ryū Shodan Manual and the about page of this website.
