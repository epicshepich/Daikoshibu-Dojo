Welcome to the Daiko Shibu Dojo's Website!

At the Daiko Shibu Dojo, we study study the traditional Eighth Light Style of jūjutsu—Hakkō Denshin Ryū. Under the guidance of Shihan Stephen Tack, students learn the self-defense techniques of the "Gentle Art."

If you wish to learn more about the Daiko Shibu Dojo or Hakkō Denshin Ryū, you can check out our about page, send us a message, or purchase your own copy of the Hakkō Denshin Ryū Shodan Manual.
